About Us

The Benton County Animal Shelter provides a loving temporary home for cats and dogs who are awaiting adoption. Stray animals can be dropped off any time the shelter is open. We would love to help you find a new pet, so please stop by to meet our animals!

Because of the limited space in our animal shelter, we are currently only able to accept dogs and cats. Fundraising efforts are underway to allow us to build a bigger space in which we can also house birds, reptiles, and small furry animals.
